How Halal Bite Works

1. Scan

Open the app, tap the scan button, and take a clear photo of the ingredient list.

2. Review & Submit

Our AI will process the image. Review the parsed ingredient list and submit for classification.

3. Get Results

Instantly see the Halal status of each ingredient, color-coded for quick understanding.
